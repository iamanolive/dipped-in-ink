about the olive

one of the most difficult and untrustworthy things about the internet (NO I'M NOT AN ENEMY. OR OVE. I LOVE THE NET AND IT'S BASICALLY KIND OF MY WHOLE ENTIRE LIFE, EXCUSE YOU) is that time when you're asked to put in an entire frickin paragraph into the world about.

YOURSELF.

(YOU ARE A DIFFICULT TOPIC TO WRITE ABOUT APPARENTLY. STOP THE WHINING)

because, like, what if this happens: “WOW I KNOW SO MUCH ABOUT ME WHERE DO I EVEN START”. (and then you never do) or maybe: “OK OK SO THIS IS AWKWARD CAN SOMEONE KINDLY TAKE OVER THE WORLD FROM ME PLEASE” (which CANNOT HAPPEN BECAUSE THE WORLD IS MINE AND I WANT IT) or maybe: “HA. YOU KNOW WHAT? I ACTUALLY HAVE SO MANY WORDS RIGHT NOW I COULD WRITE NOVELS AND FILL ALL OF THE ENTIRE WORLD WITH THEM”. and then a nosy child acidentally discovers you five centuries later, buried under papers and pens and brokenly outdated laptops, all your life-written books creating your home. AFTER WHICH YOU BECOME AN UNEARTHLY MIRACLE BECAUSE WOW YOU'VE LIVED FIVE CENTURIES. and somehow never seen most of the world. WOW YOU.

but then you know something that's worse?? (YES worse than such ridiculously idiotic achievements, bob)

that time when your previously endured torture becomes A THING O' THE PAST and you're asked to go through it once again. (THE NERVE OF THE WORLD, RIGHT?? IT'S AN EVIL PLACE)

hence the reason we find ourselves over here again, in this unnervingly feared page on the one place on earth i wholeheartedly love, a year and about 8567637 deleted drafts later. WELCOME TO THE OLIVE WORLD, FOLKS.

since you willingly clicked further than the homepage i assume you were initially directed to, we probably must start off with BEING RIDICULOUSLY PROUD OF YOUR HEROIC BRAVERY IN DOING SO. i'm impressed.

apart from that though (DON'T GET TOO USED TO THE PRAISE, OLIVE. I;M GENERALLY MUCH MORE WELL-KNOWN FOR MY CRUELLY INHUMANE WORLD RULE AROUND HERE) (you shall learn quick enough) since you've voluntarily clicked on the much-feared ABOUT ME page (I SUGGEST YOU RUN IF YOUR MIND IS EVEN THE SLGHTEST BIT IN LOVE WITH THE IDEA OF RUNNING FAR FAR AWAY AND NEVER RETURNING TO THIS OTHERWORLDLY HOME OF MINE. BECAUSE THE UNIVERSE'S LAW-ENFORCERS WON'T ALLOW ME THE LUXURY OF IMPARTING FALSE HOPE AND ENSURING YOU ALL A NEGATIVE ON ENOUNTERS WITH SUPREME TORTURE AT CERTAIN POINTS. it's a deadly place. AND I SUGGEST YOU PROTECT YOURSELF WHILE THE DOOR TO EXIT IS STILL OPEN)

BUT. if you still find yourself here after the numerous warnings (thus furthering the nature of my feared self-obsession), let's talk.

my name is anoushka and i am in love with words. i live off of chaos and pain and my heart is a dark black hollow that has never seen then light of day.

just kidding.

my name is anoushka, but they call me the olive queen because i prefer it that way. my greatest-ever ambition is to take over the world and read every single book in it. i love leigh bardugo and shall squeak pretty endlessly if you ask about her. (i have a smol little obsession like that) i am kaz brekker stan before human AND BASICALLY LOVE HIM AND SHALL HAND OVER THE WORLD if that's what he wants.

i also one day want to write a book and perhaps read every single one ever written but we're keeping the ambitions smol. BUT ONE DAY WHEN I CONQUER ALL, MY LIST WILL REACH THE MOON AND BACK.
